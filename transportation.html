Easy Ways to Travel Around the Island

Taniti offers multiple transportation options for convenient and safe travel.

Transportation in Taniti

Airport Access

Most visitors arrive through Taniti’s airport, which currently handles small jets and propeller planes.

Public Buses

Public buses operate daily in Taniti City, making local travel affordable and accessible.

Taxis

Taxis are widely available in Taniti City and provide convenient transportation to major attractions.

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available near the airport for visitors who want the freedom to explore independently.

Bikes & Walking

Taniti City is flat and walkable, with bikes and helmets available for rent around the island.

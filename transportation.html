Transportation Around Taniti

Taniti offers several convenient ways to reach the island and travel once you arrive. Most visitors arrive by air, though some come by cruise ship. Public and private ground transportation options are also available to help travelers move around safely and easily.

Travel Options

Air Travel

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Book Travel

Cruise Access

A small cruise ship visits Taniti once each week, docking in Yellow Leaf Bay and giving visitors another scenic way to experience the island.

Book Travel

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available near the airport, and private taxis also serve many parts of the island for travelers who prefer flexible transportation.

Reserve Ride

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island and can help visitors reach popular destinations.

Plan Route

Getting Around the Island

Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental bicycles are a popular choice for visitors staying close to the city center. Helmets are required by law when riding bicycles.

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable, making it easy for many visitors to explore the area on foot. Public transportation and taxis provide additional options for reaching other areas of the island.

Whether you prefer walking, biking, driving, or public transportation, Taniti offers multiple convenient ways to enjoy the island.

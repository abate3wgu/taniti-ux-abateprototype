Easy Ways to Travel Around the Island

Taniti offers multiple transportation options for convenient and safe travel.

Airport Access

Most visitors arrive by air through Taniti’s airport, which accommodates small jets and propeller planes. Expansion is planned for larger aircraft.

Public Buses

Public buses operate daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. in Taniti City, making it affordable and accessible for tourists.

Taxis

Taxis are widely available in Taniti City and provide convenient transportation to major tourist attractions.

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available near the airport for visitors who want to explore the island independently.

Bikes & Walking

Taniti City is flat and walkable. Bikes and helmets are available for rent, and helmets are required by law.

Food & Dining

Enjoy local flavors, international options, and convenient grocery access throughout Taniti.

Dining in Taniti

Taniti offers a variety of dining experiences for visitors looking to enjoy local cuisine, familiar favorites, and convenient grocery access while staying on the island.

Whether you are interested in authentic island meals, American-style dishes, or Pan-Asian flavors, Taniti provides options to suit a range of tastes and travel needs.

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Local Cuisine

Fresh fish, rice dishes, and island-inspired meals are popular with both locals and tourists.

American-Style Dining

Visitors can also enjoy familiar favorites and classic meals in several American-style restaurants.

Pan-Asian Options

Two restaurants on the island feature Pan-Asian cuisine for travelers seeking a wider variety of flavors.

Relaxed Dining Atmosphere

Many dining locations offer a welcoming island atmosphere where visitors can relax and enjoy the local experience.

Grocery Stores & Essentials

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Supermarkets

Two supermarkets on the island provide visitors with groceries, drinks, snacks, and daily essentials.

Small Grocery Stores

Smaller neighborhood grocery stores make it easy for tourists to shop close to where they stay.

24-Hour Convenience Store

A convenience store open 24 hours a day gives travelers easy access to food, drinks, and basic supplies at any time.

Quick Access to Essentials

Visitors can easily buy snacks, fruit, bottled water, and travel essentials throughout the island.

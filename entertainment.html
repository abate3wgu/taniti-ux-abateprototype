Entertainment

Discover beaches, rainforest adventures, island nightlife, and family-friendly activities across Taniti.

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do, including visiting a local history museum,

Snorkeling

Explore the waters around Taniti and enjoy colorful marine life in beautiful tropical settings.

Rainforest Zip-Lining

Enjoy thrilling zip-line adventures through the rainforest canopy for a unique island experience.

Chartered Fishing Tours

Visitors can book chartered fishing tours and enjoy island waters with experienced local guides.

Nightlife & Family Fun

Pubs & Microbrewery

Taniti offers several pubs, including a local microbrewery, for visitors looking to relax in the evening.

Dance Club

A new dance club gives visitors a lively nightlife option with music, dancing, and entertainment.

Movie Theater

Visitors can enjoy a movie night as part of a relaxing evening on the island.

Arcade & Bowling

Family-friendly entertainment includes an arcade and bowling for visitors of all ages.

More Island Experiences

  • Relax on Taniti’s beautiful beaches.
  • Explore rainforest trails and scenic island landscapes.
  • Visit the volcano for a memorable natural attraction.
  • Take helicopter rides for aerial island views.
  • Visit art galleries and discover local creativity.
  • Enjoy snorkeling and fishing tours in coastal waters.
  • Experience nightlife in Merriton Landing.
  • A nine-hole golf course is expected to open next year.

Merriton Landing

Many of Taniti’s entertainment options are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. This area continues to grow as a major destination for dining, nightlife, family entertainment, and visitor activities.

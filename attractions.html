Discover Activities & Adventures

From beaches and rainforests to cultural experiences, Taniti offers something for every traveler.

Beaches & Snorkeling

Relax on white sandy beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay and enjoy snorkeling in crystal-clear waters.

Rainforest Hiking & Zip-lining

Explore lush tropical rainforests through guided hikes and thrilling zip-lining adventures.

Active Volcano Tours

Visit Taniti’s mountainous interior and safely experience views of the island’s active volcano.

Local History Museum

Learn about Taniti’s indigenous culture, history, and traditions at the island’s museum.

Fishing & Boat Tours

Enjoy chartered fishing tours, sightseeing cruises, and island boat excursions.

Entertainment & Nightlife

Visit pubs, a microbrewery, dance club, arcade, art galleries, and a movie theater in Merriton Landing.

Golf Course (Coming Soon)

Play at the new nine-hole golf course being developed near Merriton Landing.

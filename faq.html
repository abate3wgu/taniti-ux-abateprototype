Travel Information for Visitors

Helpful answers for planning your trip to Taniti Island.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of power outlets are used in Taniti?

Taniti uses standard 120-volt outlets, similar to those used in the United States.

Is English widely spoken?

Many younger Taniti residents speak fluent English, especially in tourist areas.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare, though travelers should still use normal precautions in busy areas.

What currency is used?

The U.S. dollar is widely accepted on the island, and major credit cards are commonly used.

Are there medical services available?

Yes. Taniti has one hospital and several clinics available for residents and visitors.

Do I need to reserve activities in advance?

It is recommended, especially during busy travel periods. Use the reservation page to plan ahead.

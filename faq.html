Important Information for Visitors

Everything you need to know before traveling to Taniti Island.

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its primary currency. Many businesses also accept major credit cards and some foreign currencies.

What is the power outlet standard?

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same standard used in the United States, so most U.S. travelers will not need an adapter.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare on the island. However, visitors should be aware of occasional pickpocketing in busy tourist areas.

Do people speak English in Taniti?

Many younger residents and workers in tourist areas speak fluent English, though less English is spoken in rural regions.

Are there hospitals and medical services?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics with multilingual staff available to assist tourists.

Are there any travel restrictions or local laws?

Alcohol is not sold or served between midnight and 9:00 a.m. Visitors should also be aware that some businesses close on national holidays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful visitor information for planning a safe and enjoyable trip to Taniti Island.

Visitor Information

Power Outlets

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Alcohol Sales

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Drinking Age

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and is not strictly enforced.

Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, though very little English is spoken in rural areas by older residents.

Medical Services

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Safety

Violent crime is very rare, though pickpocketing and other petty crimes have increased with tourism.

Holiday Closures

Many attractions and restaurants close on national holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

Currency

Taniti uses U.S. dollars, but some businesses also accept euros and yen.
